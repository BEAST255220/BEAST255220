About Kerala Logistics

Kerala Logistics, a newly launched virtual trucking company in TruckersMP, brings a fresh approach to the digital highways of the simulation world. With a focus on professionalism and camaraderie, Kerala Logistics aims to carve its path in the virtual transportation industry. Leveraging the latest technology and a passion for excellence, the company endeavors to provide a platform for trucking enthusiasts to come together, participate in convoys, and foster a vibrant community spirit. Join Kerala Logistics on the digital roads, where every journey is an adventure.
